# NoaChess

A 6×6 chess variant with unique piece movements, powered by Fairy-Stockfish.

## About

NoaChess features:
- **6×6 board**
- **Unique pieces**: Leaper, modified Bishop/Rook/Queen, standard Knight and King
- **Pawn promotion**: Pawns promote to Generals (non-royal kings)
- **Starting position**: LBQKNR (mirrored)

## Play Online

Visit: https://noa-chess.vercel.app

## Piece Movements

- **Pawn**: Moves forward/sideways, captures diagonally forward
- **Bishop**: Moves 1 diagonal OR jumps 2 orthogonal
- **Rook**: Moves up to 4 squares orthogonally
- **Queen**: Moves up to 4 squares in any direction
- **Leaper**: Moves 1 square any direction, captures by jumping 2
- **Knight**: Standard L-shape movement
- **King**: Standard king (royal piece)
- **General**: Moves 1 square any direction (promoted pawn, non-royal)
